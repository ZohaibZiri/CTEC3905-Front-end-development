Italian Pasta Products

Tagliatelle

Tagliatelle, typical of Emilian cuisine and the middle region of Italy is exquisite with all types of sauces( white or red) featuring fish or meat. It’s an excellent choice for making “sformati,” or molded preparations in Italian cuisine.

Tagliolini

Tagliolini is thinner than classic tagliatelle and features well in lighter dishes: with fish-based sauces, “in bianco” (served simply with butter and a grating of parmesan) or even in broth.

Spaghetti Grossi

The first Wholewheat Pasta worldwide with a certificate of cooking performance.Impressively “al dente” after cooking, Spaghetti Grossi absorbs sauces with its porous surface and is therefore an ideal partner for the decisive flavors found in ragùs based on meat or shellfish.

Paccheri Italiani

Although its name comes from a word that means "to slap," Paccheri is a perfect example of the pleasure of Neapolitan pasta tradition. It's beautifully changed to fit seafood recipes and full-bodied, meat-based sauces.

Sugo Al Basilico

100% rich Italian tomatoes in a fine pulp are blended smoothly with certified, genuine Genovese basil (PDO) to bring you one of the great Italian flavor combinations.

Pesto Alla Genovse

We combine flavorful Italian basil with the right touch of garlic and certified authentic Grana Padano and Pecorino Romano cheeses for a pesto faithful to Ligurian tradition.
